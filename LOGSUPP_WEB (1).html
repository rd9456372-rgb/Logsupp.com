    02Waiting Time & Loading/Unloading Charges

    Free waiting time is provided at each collection and delivery point. After the free period, waiting charges apply per hour (or part thereof), covering driver time while waiting for loading, unloading, or access at your premises.

    Vehicle TypeFree Waiting TimeCharge After Free Period
    Small Van, SWB, MWB, LWB, XLWB30 min per collection/drop£25 per hour
    Luton Box / Luton + Tail Lift30 min per collection/drop£35 per hour
    7.5t Truck and above45 min per collection/drop£50 per hour

    Waiting time is calculated from the moment the driver arrives at the collection or delivery point. Part hours are charged as full hours.

    Handball & Manual Loading Charges — all handball, manual loading, and unloading assistance is chargeable, including:

    • Carrying items to or from floors above/below ground level
    • Loading or unloading heavy or bulky items manually
    • Navigating goods through restricted access areas
    • Any physical handling beyond tailgate/kerbside

    Handball charges will be confirmed before the service begins or added to the final invoice where assistance is requested on-site.

    05Cancellations & Refund Policy

    Cancellation by Customer

    • Cancellations made more than 1 hour before the scheduled collection time are eligible for a full refund
    • Cancellations made within 1 hour of collection may incur a cancellation fee of up to 50% of the booking value
    • Once a vehicle has been dispatched and is en route, the full booking fee is payable

    Refunds for Online Pay-As-You-Go Bookings — the refund amount depends on the stage of service:

    • Before vehicle dispatch: full refund
    • Vehicle dispatched but not yet arrived: refund minus dispatch fee
    • Vehicle arrived / goods collected: no refund (service in progress)

    All eligible refunds are processed from our side within 24 hours. Please allow 3–5 working days for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.

    Cancellation by Logsupp — in rare circumstances we may need to cancel a booking (e.g. no vehicle available, severe weather, road closures). In such cases you will receive a full refund processed within 24 hours.

    06Liability & Insurance

    Logsupp carries Goods in Transit insurance and Public Liability insurance.

    • Standard Goods in Transit cover is included with every booking
    • For high-value items, additional insurance must be discussed and arranged before booking
    • Claims must be submitted in writing within 7 days of delivery
    • We are not liable for loss or damage caused by inadequate packaging by the sender
    • Our total liability shall not exceed the value declared at the time of booking or the cost of the delivery service, whichever is lower

    Logsupp is not liable for any indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages.

    07Restricted & Prohibited Goods

    You may not use our services to transport:

    • Illegal substances or items
    • Dangerous goods without proper ADR certification and prior arrangement
    • Counterfeit or stolen goods
    • Weapons, firearms, or explosives
    • Live animals (unless specifically arranged)
    • Items that could cause harm to people, property, or the vehicle
    • Perishable goods without appropriate refrigerated transport being booked

    We reserve the right to refuse any consignment that we believe poses a risk. If restricted goods are found after collection, additional charges and legal action may apply.

    08Customer Responsibilities
    • Ensure all goods are properly packaged, labelled, and ready for collection at the agreed time
    • Provide accurate weight and dimensions; underestimating may result in additional charges or service refusal
    • Ensure safe and legal access for our vehicle at both collection and delivery points
    • Inform us of any parking restrictions, loading bay requirements, or access limitations in advance
    • Provide any necessary documentation (customs forms, delivery notes, etc.)
    • Ensure a responsible person is available to hand over and receive the goods
    09Proof of Delivery

    We obtain proof of delivery (POD) for every consignment, which may include a signature, photograph, or named recipient confirmation. POD records are retained for 12 months and available upon request.
